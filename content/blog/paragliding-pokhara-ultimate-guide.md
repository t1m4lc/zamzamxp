## Why Pokhara is a World-Class Paragliding Destination

**Pokhara** is consistently ranked among the top 5 paragliding destinations in the world. With its perfect combination of thermal conditions, stunning Himalayan backdrop, and lakeside landing zones, it offers an unparalleled flying experience for both beginners and experienced pilots.

### What Makes Pokhara Special

- **Dramatic scenery:** Fly with views of Annapurna, Machhapuchhare (Fishtail), and Dhaulagiri
- **Reliable thermals:** Consistent flying conditions 300+ days per year
- **Safe landing zone:** Open lakeside area at Phewa Lake
- **Altitude range:** Launch from 1,500m, soar to 3,000m+ on good days
- **Affordable:** Some of the best value paragliding in the world

## Types of Paragliding Flights

### Tandem Flight (Beginners)

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Duration** | 20-30 minutes (standard) or 45-60 minutes (extended) |
| **Experience needed** | None - pilot does everything |
| **Cost** | $70-120 USD |
| **Includes** | Photos and video usually extra ($20-30) |

Perfect for first-timers. You fly with a licensed pilot who handles takeoff, flying, and landing. Your job is to enjoy the views!

### Solo Paragliding (Licensed Pilots)

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Requirements** | Paragliding license + insurance |
| **Equipment** | Bring your own or rent locally |
| **Cost** | Sarangkot transport + optional guide pilot |

Experienced pilots can fly independently with proper documentation.

### Paragliding Courses

| Course | Duration | Cost |
|--------|----------|------|
| **Discovery course** | 3-5 days | $400-600 |
| **P1-P2 certification** | 10-14 days | $1,000-1,500 |

Learn to fly with certified instructors in ideal conditions.

## The Flight Experience

### Takeoff: Sarangkot

Your adventure begins at **Sarangkot** (1,592m), a ridge overlooking Pokhara and the Annapurna range. 

**What to expect:**
1. Short briefing on running technique
2. Harness fitting and safety check
3. 5-10 step run down the grassy slope
4. Smooth lift-off as thermals catch the wing

### In the Air

Once airborne, you'll experience:
- **Thermal riding:** Circling in rising warm air to gain altitude
- **Panoramic views:** 360° views of Himalayas, Phewa Lake, and Pokhara
- **Photo opportunities:** Pilots often take in-flight photos/videos
- **Acrobatics (optional):** Spirals and wing-overs for thrill-seekers

### Landing: Lakeside

The flight concludes with a gentle landing at the lakeside landing zone near Phewa Lake, just steps from restaurants and cafes.

## Best Time for Paragliding

| Season | Months | Rating | Conditions |
|--------|--------|--------|------------|
| **Autumn** | Oct-Nov | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Best thermals, clear skies, stunning views |
| **Winter** | Dec-Feb | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Clear views, cooler air, shorter flights |
| **Spring** | Mar-May | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Good thermals, some haze, longer flights |
| **Monsoon** | Jun-Sep | ⭐⭐ | Limited flying, rain delays, cloudy |

### Daily Timing

- **Morning flights (8-10 AM):** Calmer air, beginner-friendly
- **Midday flights (11 AM-2 PM):** Strongest thermals, longest flights
- **Evening flights (3-5 PM):** Softer light, romantic sunset views

## Safety & What to Wear

### Safety Record

Pokhara has an excellent safety record due to:
- Strict licensing requirements for pilots
- Regular equipment inspections
- Well-established flight paths
- Reliable weather monitoring

### What to Wear

| Item | Recommendation |
|------|----------------|
| **Footwear** | Closed-toe shoes (sneakers ideal) |
| **Clothing** | Long pants recommended |
| **Layers** | Light jacket (cooler at altitude) |
| **Sunglasses** | Secure strap recommended |
| **Camera** | Securely attached only |

### Not Recommended

❌ Sandals or flip-flops  
❌ Loose scarves or jewelry  
❌ Heavy backpacks  
❌ If pregnant or with serious back/heart conditions

## Booking Tips

1. **Book 1-2 days in advance** in peak season (October-November)
2. **Morning slots** fill up first - book early
3. **Weather delays** are common - build flexibility into your schedule
4. **Compare operators** - ask about pilot experience and equipment age
5. **Check reviews** - look for recent testimonials
