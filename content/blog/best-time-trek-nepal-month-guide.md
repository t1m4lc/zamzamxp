## When is the Best Time to Trek in Nepal?

**Short answer:** October-November (autumn) and March-May (spring) are the prime trekking seasons in Nepal. But each month has its advantages depending on what you're looking for.

This guide breaks down every month so you can plan the perfect trek based on weather, crowds, views, and your personal preferences.

## Planning Tips

### For Best Weather: October
- Book 2-3 months in advance
- Expect crowds
- Be flexible with itinerary

### For Rhododendrons: Late March - Early April
- Lower Annapurna and Langtang best
- Morning treks for clearest views
- Carry rain layer

### For Solitude: December - February
- Choose lower altitude treks
- Bring serious cold weather gear
- Check pass conditions before going

### For Budget Travelers: Shoulder Seasons
- Late November or early March
- Good conditions, fewer crowds
- Better lodge availability
