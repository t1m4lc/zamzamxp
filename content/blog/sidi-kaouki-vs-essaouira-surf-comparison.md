## Two Great Surf Spots, One Region

The **Essaouira region** offers two distinct surf experiences just 25 km apart. Whether you're a beginner looking for mellow waves or an intermediate surfer seeking more power, understanding the differences will help you choose the perfect spot.

---

## Quick Comparison

| Factor | Sidi Kaouki | Essaouira Bay |
|--------|-------------|---------------|
| **Distance from Essaouira** | 25 km south | In the city |
| **Wave Type** | Beach break, more power | Beach/point break, mellow |
| **Best Level** | Intermediate to advanced | Beginner to intermediate |
| **Crowd Level** | Low | Moderate |
| **Wind Protection** | Less protected | More protected |
| **Vibe** | Chill village | City amenities |
| **Accommodation** | Budget guesthouses | Full range |

---

## Sidi Kaouki

### The Wave

Sidi Kaouki is a long, exposed beach break that picks up more swell than Essaouira Bay.

| Wave Characteristic | Details |
|---------------------|---------|
| **Type** | Beach break with multiple peaks |
| **Direction** | Works best on NW-W swells |
| **Size Range** | 2-8 ft (can hold bigger) |
| **Best Tide** | Mid to high tide |
| **Bottom** | Sandy |
| **Wave Length** | Medium rides, 50-100m possible |

### Who It's Best For

✅ **Intermediate surfers** looking to progress  
✅ **Those seeking fewer crowds**  
✅ **Travelers wanting a village atmosphere**  
✅ **Budget-conscious surfers**  
✅ **Kiters and windsurfers** (afternoon winds)  

### Typical Day at Sidi Kaouki

| Time | Conditions |
|------|------------|
| **Dawn - 10 AM** | Best surfing, light winds |
| **10 AM - 2 PM** | Wind building, still surfable |
| **2 PM onwards** | Strong wind, kite/windsurf conditions |

### Pros and Cons

| Pros | Cons |
|------|------|
| ✅ More consistent waves | ❌ More exposed to wind |
| ✅ Fewer crowds | ❌ Less infrastructure |
| ✅ Authentic village feel | ❌ 30-min transport to Essaouira |
| ✅ Budget-friendly | ❌ Can be powerful for beginners |
| ✅ Beautiful, wide beach | ❌ Limited nightlife |

---

## Essaouira Bay

### The Wave

Essaouira Bay offers a more sheltered setup with multiple breaks along the beach and a point break on good swells.

| Wave Characteristic | Details |
|---------------------|---------|
| **Type** | Beach break + point break |
| **Direction** | NW-W swells, protected from wind |
| **Size Range** | 1-5 ft (rarely bigger) |
| **Best Tide** | All tides, best at mid |
| **Bottom** | Sandy (beach), rocky (point) |
| **Wave Length** | Short to medium rides |

### Who It's Best For

✅ **Beginners** learning to surf  
✅ **Intermediate surfers** on smaller days  
✅ **Families** with mixed skill levels  
✅ **Travelers wanting city amenities**  
✅ **Those combining surf with culture**  

### Typical Day at Essaouira Bay

| Time | Conditions |
|------|------------|
| **Dawn - 11 AM** | Clean conditions, best for surf |
| **11 AM - 3 PM** | Wind affecting, still workable |
| **3 PM onwards** | Kite/windsurf dominated |

### Pros and Cons

| Pros | Cons |
|------|------|
| ✅ More wind-protected | ❌ Smaller waves usually |
| ✅ City amenities | ❌ More crowded |
| ✅ Multiple breaks to choose | ❌ Less consistent in summer |
| ✅ Great for beginners | ❌ Can feel busy |
| ✅ Easy board rental | ❌ Less "surf village" feel |

## Our Recommendation

### Choose Sidi Kaouki If:

- You're an **intermediate surfer** wanting progression
- You prefer a **quiet, village atmosphere**
- You're on a **tight budget**
- You want to **escape the crowds**
- You're interested in **kitesurfing/windsurfing** too

### Choose Essaouira Bay If:

- You're a **beginner** learning to surf
- You want **city amenities** (restaurants, cafés, nightlife)
- You're traveling with **family or mixed-level group**
- You want to **combine surf with cultural sightseeing**
- You prefer **easy logistics**

### Choose Both (Best Option)!

The spots are close enough that you can easily surf both during your trip:

| Day | Morning | Afternoon |
|-----|---------|-----------|
| Small swell | Essaouira Bay | Explore medina |
| Big swell | Sidi Kaouki | Essaouira café |
| Windy day | Either (kite/windsurf) | Hammam, shopping |
